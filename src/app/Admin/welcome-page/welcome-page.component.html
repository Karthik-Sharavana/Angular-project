Welcome To Meeting Application

This page will teach how to use this application, and what are the steps to be taken while registering the room.

Here are the steps to register the room

  • Fill all the details present in the Fill Room Details form, else you cannot be processed further
  • After filling all the details in Fill Room Details form, click next button, which is available below the form.
  • Fill all the details present in the Address form.
  • After filling all the details in Address form, click next button.
  • This will be the last form to fill the details, where all the details regarding equipments needed in the room.
    • Select the options what are all the equipments provided during meeting.
  • The Last step is to Register all the detaisl entered.
